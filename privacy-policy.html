Veyrazo Privacy Policy

Veyrazo is a fitness and wellness app from Sloth Bloom. It helps users create an account, complete onboarding, build adaptive home and outdoor routines, track health and workout progress, use widgets, connect a Wear OS watch, view guided workout links, and use optional social and reward features.

This policy explains what data Veyrazo may collect, store, sync, process, and share when those features are used.

Account And Security Data

Veyrazo may collect or store the following when needed for the selected feature:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Birthday
  • Profile photo when uploaded by the user
  • Authentication status
  • Session status
  • Email verification status
  • Trusted-device status
  • Two-factor security preferences
  • Security questions and user-provided security answers
  • Account recovery challenge status and related verification state

This data is used to create and secure the account, support sign-in by email or username, restore account data across devices, support password and username recovery, support email-code verification, trusted-device checks, and two-factor security, protect sensitive account actions, and display safe public profile details for friends, friend challenges, and leaderboard features.

Onboarding And Fitness Profile Data

Veyrazo may collect onboarding, routine-planning, and preference details such as your goals, activity level, limitations, workout schedule, preferred routine style, workout duration, hydration-related inputs, daily challenge choices, and similar profile data.

This data is used to personalize routines, daily challenges, guided suggestions, recovery-safe exercise choices, hydration goals, coaching context, and dashboard summaries.

Health, Fitness, And Activity Data

When the related features are used, Veyrazo may collect, store, sync, or process:

  • Workout history and exercise completion
  • Routine history and adherence
  • Hydration logs and goals
  • Weight logs
  • Step data
  • Calories and activity estimates
  • Outdoor event summaries such as distance, duration, pace, speed, elevation, and route points
  • Heart-rate data when explicitly shared through Health Connect or a connected wearable source
  • Other fitness or wellness progress data the user chooses to track

This data is used to power live stats, progress charts, widgets, adaptive routines, challenge completion, coaching guidance, and optional Wear OS sync.

Location Data

When outdoor tracking, weather, or route-based features are used, Veyrazo may process approximate location and precise location.

This data is used for outdoor event tracking, route summaries, location-based weather conditions, map display, target guidance, and related safety or session features.

Photos And Profile Media

If you choose to update your profile photo or use image-related profile features, Veyrazo may process photos that you select or capture.

Photos are used only for the account or social profile features you choose to use.

Messages, Notifications, And In-App Content

Veyrazo may generate or store in-app messages, reminders, notification content, challenge prompts, coaching responses, update notices, and other user-facing content needed to operate the app.

Social And Community Features

If you use friend requests, challenges, shared profile cards, or leaderboard features, Veyrazo may process friend codes, request state, challenge status, safe summary stats, selected display profile details, and leaderboard-related progress totals.

Private health details such as full route history, body details, hydration logs, or similar private records are not intended for broad public sharing through these social surfaces.

Ads, Rewards, And Diagnostics

Veyrazo includes ads and may use advertising or analytics-related SDK signals such as device or other identifiers, diagnostics, crash logs, ad interaction state, reward state, and similar app-performance data.

This data is used to support banners, interstitials, rewarded features, app stability, diagnostics, fraud prevention, consent flows, and ad-related measurement where applicable.

How Data May Be Stored Or Shared

Storage

Data may be stored locally on your device and, for signed-in features, in connected backend services such as Firebase services used by the app.

Sharing

Data may be shared with service providers or SDK partners needed to operate features such as authentication, sync, crash reporting, notifications, analytics, maps, weather, ads, consent handling, and wearable integrations.

Your Controls

Depending on the feature, you may be able to update or remove profile details, sign out, disable optional permissions, adjust security settings, remove photos, reset or change onboarding-related settings, manage privacy choices, and request account deletion.

Account And Data Deletion

If you want your Veyrazo account and account-linked data deleted, use the dedicated deletion page at https://surakage.github.io/account-deletion.html.

That page explains how to request deletion by email and how to delete the account from inside the app when available.
