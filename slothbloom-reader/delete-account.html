Slothbloom Reader Account And Data Deletion

Slothbloom Reader primarily stores your reading data locally on your device. This page explains how to remove local app data and how to request deletion of any account-linked or cloud-linked data if that applies to your use of the app.

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Delete Local App Data On Your Device

If you only used Slothbloom Reader locally, you can remove your reading data directly from the device:

  1. Open Slothbloom Reader.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Use the local reset or data-clearing controls if available.
  4. You can also remove the app and clear its storage from Android system app settings.

This will remove locally stored readings, saved study content, preferences, and similar device-only app data.

Request Deletion Of Account-Linked Or Cloud-Linked Data

If you used a cloud-linked, account-linked, support-linked, or purchase-support workflow and want any associated data reviewed for deletion, send a request to support@slothbloom.com.

Please include:

  • Your email address used for the request or related support contact
  • The app name: Slothbloom Reader
  • Your request type: local data question, account deletion, or cloud-data deletion
  • Any helpful details that identify the data to remove

What Happens After A Deletion Request

We will review the request and remove account-linked or cloud-linked data that is reasonably tied to your request and not required to be retained for legal, billing, fraud-prevention, security, or operational reasons.

Some records, such as transaction, tax, chargeback, fraud-prevention, or basic support logs, may need to be retained for a limited period where required.

Subscription And Purchase Notes

Deleting local or account-linked app data does not automatically cancel a Google Play subscription. If you need to cancel Reader Plus, do that through your Google Play subscriptions page. One-time credit pack purchases are handled through Google Play billing records.

Questions

If you are not sure whether your data is only local or includes account-linked support or purchase information, contact support@slothbloom.com and we can help you identify the right deletion path.
