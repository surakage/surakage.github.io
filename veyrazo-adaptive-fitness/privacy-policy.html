Veyrazo Privacy Policy

Veyrazo is a fitness and wellness app from Sloth Bloom. It helps users create an account, complete onboarding, build adaptive home and outdoor routines, track health and workout progress, use widgets, connect a Wear OS watch, view guided workout links, and use optional social and reward features.

This policy explains what data Veyrazo may collect, store, sync, process, and share when those features are used.

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Overview

Veyrazo may process account, onboarding, activity, routine, progress, social, notification, wearable, and optional advertising-related data to operate the app and its connected features.

Need to delete your Veyrazo account or account-linked data? Use the dedicated deletion page: Veyrazo Account Deletion.

Account And Security Data

  • Username, email address, display name, birthday, and optional profile photo
  • Authentication state, session state, email verification state, and trusted-device state
  • Security-question setup and related recovery or verification state

This data is used to create and secure the account, support sign-in and recovery, restore account data across devices, and protect sensitive account actions.

Fitness, Routine, And Progress Data

  • Goals, activity level, height, weight when provided, workout days, routine length, active days, and limitations
  • Workout completions, hydration logs, steps, progress summaries, recovery items, and coach-related exercise history
  • Outdoor activity summaries, route-related session data, and compatible wearable sync data when those features are used

This data is used to personalize routines, support tracking and progress summaries, improve routine safety, and maintain cross-device continuity between phone and Wear OS where applicable.

Health Connect, Wearables, And Notifications

When enabled by the user, Veyrazo may use compatible Health Connect reads and supported wearable flows for items such as steps, activity totals, exercise sessions, and heart-rate-related data.

If notification permission is granted, Veyrazo may show reminders, workout prompts, social alerts, and summary notifications.

Advertising, Analytics, And Cloud Services

Veyrazo may use Firebase and related Google services for authentication, storage, sync, app reliability, and abuse prevention. Veyrazo may also use Google advertising services where enabled in the phone app.

Veyrazo does not sell personal data.

User Controls And Contact

Users can sign out, update account details, reset local progress, manage connected permissions in Android settings, and delete their account through the in-app account flow.

For privacy questions or account and data deletion requests, contact veyrazo.review@gmail.com.
