Veyrazo Account Deletion

This page explains how to delete your Veyrazo account and what data is removed when the deletion request is completed.

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Delete Your Account In The App

  1. Open Veyrazo.
  2. Go to the account or settings area that contains account management.
  3. Choose the delete-account option.
  4. Complete any required security or confirmation steps.

If extra verification is required, follow the in-app prompts before the deletion request can finish.

What Is Removed

When account deletion completes, Veyrazo is designed to remove the Firebase Authentication account, private Firestore account data, public profile data, username reservation data owned by that account, and profile-photo storage tied to that account, subject to normal security, fraud-prevention, backup, and legal-retention limits.

Need Help

If you cannot access the app or need help with a deletion request, contact veyrazo.review@gmail.com.
