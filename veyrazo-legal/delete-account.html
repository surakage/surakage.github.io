Veyrazo Account And Data Deletion

This page explains how a Veyrazo user can request deletion of an account and the account-linked data associated with it.

Use this page if you need the Google Play account deletion URL for Veyrazo, want to submit a deletion request outside the app, or want to understand what is removed when an account is deleted.

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Request Deletion From The Web

If you cannot access the app or prefer to submit a web-based request, email veyrazo.review@gmail.com with the subject line Veyrazo account deletion request.

Please include the account email address used in Veyrazo, the username if it is different from the email, and a short note confirming that you want your account and associated account-linked data deleted.

For account security, Sloth Bloom may ask for limited verification before completing the deletion request.

Delete Your Account In The App

  1. Open Veyrazo and sign in to the account you want to delete.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Tap the gear icon.
  4. Open Settings.
  5. Go to the Account area.
  6. Tap Permanently delete account.
  7. Complete any required sign-in confirmation so Veyrazo can verify the request and finish deletion safely.

What Gets Deleted

When a Veyrazo account deletion request is completed, Veyrazo is designed to remove the sign-in account and the account-linked records tied to that account, including:

  • Private profile data
  • Public profile records created for social features
  • Username reservations
  • Uploaded account files such as account-linked profile image storage
  • Synced account records stored for routines, progress, social, and related app features

What This Does Not Cover

Veyrazo currently treats account deletion as the main deletion request flow for account-linked cloud data. Resetting progress is separate from deleting the account.

  • Reset progress clears supported progress-related data while keeping the account.
  • Delete account removes the account and the account-linked private and public records associated with it.

Retention And Limited Exceptions

Some technical or compliance-related records may be retained for a limited time when reasonably necessary for security, fraud prevention, service integrity, legal compliance, or to complete the deletion workflow itself.

Veyrazo does not keep an active user account available after a completed account deletion request finishes successfully.

Response Timing

Deletion requests are reviewed and completed as quickly as reasonably possible after account ownership is verified.

If you are contacting Sloth Bloom through the email path above, keep your request details in the same email thread so the request can be processed more quickly.
