Veyrazo Account And Data Deletion

This page explains how Veyrazo users can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How To Request Deletion

Email suraruiso.creations@gmail.com from the email address linked to your Veyrazo account.

Please include your Veyrazo username and the email address tied to the account so ownership can be verified before deletion is processed.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Account profile details linked to the Veyrazo account
  • Synced onboarding and routine data
  • Progress, hydration, weight, workout, challenge, and social profile data tied to that account
  • Stored profile photo content and other account-linked cloud records

What May Be Retained Temporarily

Limited records may be retained for security, fraud prevention, backups, and legal compliance for the minimum time reasonably required.

In-App Deletion

If deletion is requested through the in-app account removal flow, the same account and cloud-data deletion process applies.

Support

For questions about deletion or privacy, contact veyrazo.review@gmail.com.
