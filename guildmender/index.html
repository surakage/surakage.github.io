Getting help

Server owners and administrators should first confirm that GuildMender is online, has the permissions requested by the command, and is positioned above any roles it must manage. For privacy, deletion, or legal questions, contact support@slothbloom.com.

GuildMender is an independent Sloth Bloom project and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Discord or Twitch.
